About me

My name is Gerard Degas and I am a web developer.

I grew up in a household with a French father and learned to speak French and think in other languages at a very young age. Since then I developed a love for learning languages and have since also learned to speak Spanish.

After having received my Bachelor's of Arts in Political Science, I decided to become a school teacher which I did in both France where I taught English and here at a private high school, where I taught history and French. After a few years I began to realize teaching didn't provide the challenge that I was looking for. I loved learning languages and was always very good with computers and so I found coding, a perfect fit.

I then enrolled in Orange County Code School where I learned how to program web applications and graduated in August of 2017. I am now capable of developing full stack applications, and have done so on many occasions as can be seen on my many personal projects.

I am currently looking for a full time position as a developer and am best reached through E-mail at gdegas001@gmail.com
