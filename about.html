About me

I'm Gerard Degas and I am a web developer.

I grew up learning both French and English as my first languages in elementary school in Fountain Valley, CA. I started to think in other languages at a very young age. Since then I developed a love for learning languages as well as teaching them and have always been good at picking them up.

After having received my degree in poli-sci at UC Riverside, I decided to become a school teacher. I taught English in Bordeaux, France for a little while and then moved back to the US to teach private high school languages, where I taught social sciences and French, but something was missing. After a few years I started to feel something lacked in my career in teaching, I wasn't being challenged the way I wanted to be. I knew I loved learning languages and was always adept working with computers and so I found coding.

I enrolled in Orange County Code School where I learned how to program web applications and graduated in August of 2017. I now love my work as a full stack engineer at Uber, building awesome web applications every day.

I love continuing to grow and develop my network and can be best reach through email gdegas001@gmail.com. Also, check out my blog!
